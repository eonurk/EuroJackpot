The game, in one paragraph

Pick 5 numbers from 50, add 2 Euro numbers from 12, and match all seven to win a jackpot that starts at €10 million and climbs to a €120 million cap. Draws happen every Tuesday and Friday in Helsinki, across 18 countries. Since launch the game has quietly changed its own rules twice — a detail that, as you’ll see, quietly breaks most casual analyses of it.

How we work

Descriptive statistics, formal hypothesis tests, and walk-forward backtests — visualised, and honest about uncertainty. Where a popular belief fails a test, we say so; where the data genuinely contains structure (it happens!), we show where it comes from.

Play-it-straight note: lottery draws are independent random events. Past results cannot predict future outcomes, and this site will show you precisely why — with tests, not slogans. Everything here is for education and curiosity. If you play, treat the ticket as entertainment, never as an investment.
